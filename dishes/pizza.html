My Pizza Recipe

Description

Margherita Pizza is a timeless Italian classic that celebrates simplicity and quality ingredients. With a thin, crispy crust, it's adorned with a vibrant trio of flavors - ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves. The result is a harmonious marriage of tangy, sweet, and creamy notes, creating a pizza that's as visually stunning as it is delicious. Each bite is a journey through the essence of Italian cuisine, capturing the essence of a warm summer day in Naples.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps To Follow

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can reach (usually around 500°F or 260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven during preheating

  2. Prepare the Dough:

    • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into your desired thickness. If you prefer a thinner crust, roll it out more. For a thicker crust, leave it a bit thicker.

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. If using a pizza stone, ensure it's well-floured or use a pizza peel with a bit of cornmeal to prevent sticking.

  4. Prepare the Dough:

    • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into your desired thickness. If you prefer a thinner crust, roll it out more. For a thicker crust, leave it a bit thicker.

  5. Add the Sauce:

    • Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

  6. Add the Cheese:

    • Arrange the slices or pieces of fresh mozzarella evenly over the sauce. Make sure it covers the pizza evenly.

  7. Sprinkle with Basil:

    • Place fresh basil leaves on top of the cheese. You can use them whole or tear them into smaller pieces for a more rustic look.

  8. Drizzle with Olive Oil:

    • Drizzle a bit of extra-virgin olive oil over the pizza. This adds flavor and helps to brown the crust.

  9. Season and Optional Cheese:

    • Season with a pinch of salt and pepper. If desired, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese on top for an extra cheesy kick.

  10. Bake the Pizza:

    • Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or place the baking sheet in the oven. Bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  11. Serve:

    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy!

This Margherita pizza is a true Italian classic, and its simple elegance allows the quality of the ingredients to shine. Buon appetito!
